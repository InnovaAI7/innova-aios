## Delivery Process (AI Adoption Roadmap)

**Phase 1 — Discovery**
1. Initial Call — understand client interest, overview of capabilities
2. Exploration Meeting — map workflows, identify automation opportunities
3. Scope of Work (SOW) Definition — full technical/operational scope, features, timelines, terms
4. Client Approval & Agreement — sign-off before build begins

**Phase 2 — Build**
5. Beta Build — functional prototype with all core functionality
6. Client Demonstration — live testing with client team
7. Feedback Loop — refinement until ready for deployment

**Phase 3 — Launch & Support**
8. Final Implementation — production-ready deployment integrated into client systems
9. Training, Sign Off & Hypercare — tailored training session, documentation, then 30-day hypercare monitoring
10. Ongoing Maintenance & Support (optional) — continuous monitoring, updates, priority technical support under agreed SLA

## Case Studies / Success Stories

- **Franchise Recruitment Platform** — Branded franchise recruitment website with embedded AI chatbot across all pages. Answers questions in real time, captures applicant data, generates lead summaries, routes by region, stores transcripts. Result: automated franchise acquisition funnel with improved lead quality.

- **Property Portfolio Intelligence Platform** — Custom dashboard for a landlord managing single lets, HMOs, and short-term rentals. Consolidates certificate tracking (with expiry alerts), cash flow tracking, tenant records, subcontractor management, AI financial analysis, compliance notifications. Result: replaced fragmented spreadsheets with a centralised intelligence system.

- **AI-Powered LinkedIn Outreach Agent** — Automation agent that connects with ICP-aligned prospects, engages with profiles/content, sends structured multi-step DMs, identifies and verifies email addresses, sends follow-up warm emails. Result: consistent automated outbound pipeline engine.

- **Tenant Communication & Booking Automation** — Platform for a construction company managing tenant comms across multiple languages and channels (SMS, WhatsApp, email, phone). Books directly into company calendars, answers queries via AI, sends automated reminders, provides internal dashboard for campaigns/bookings/team management. Result: reduced admin burden, improved booking efficiency.
